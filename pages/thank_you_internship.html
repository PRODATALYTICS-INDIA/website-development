What Happens Next?

Our comprehensive selection process ensures we identify the most promising data science talent. Here's your roadmap to joining our elite internship program.

1

Application Review

Our expert team reviews your application, portfolio, and academic background. We evaluate technical skills, passion for data science, and alignment with program objectives.

Timeline: 5-7 business days
2

Technical Assessment

Shortlisted candidates complete a comprehensive technical assessment covering Python, statistics, data analysis, and problem-solving skills relevant to real-world data science projects.

Duration: 2-3 hours online assessment
3

Interview Round

Final candidates participate in a structured interview with our senior data scientists. We discuss your background, career goals, and assess cultural fit with our team.

Format: 45-minute video interview
4

Final Decision

Selected candidates receive program confirmation with onboarding details, curriculum overview, and pre-program preparation materials to ensure success.

Notification: Within 48 hours of interview
