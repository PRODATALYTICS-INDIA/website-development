About The Program

Our Data Science and ML Specialist Internship Program is designed for individuals with basic mathematical, statistical, or computer science knowledge who want to launch their careers in Data Science and Machine Learning. The program offers expert-led classes from both industry professionals and academicians, with a 100% online format for flexibility. Participants will benefit from interactive doubt-clearing sessions and gain hands-on experience by working on more than five real data projects, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios and develop practical skills for the job market.

Course Overview

1. Introduction to Data Science

Data Science is the art of transforming raw data into valuable insights through analytical and computational techniques.

2. Introduction to Data Analysis

Data analysis is the process of inspecting, cleaning, and modeling data to uncover useful information, draw conclusions, and support decision-making.

3. Machine Learning Fundamentals

Machine Learning is a core branch of AI that enables systems to learn from data and improve over time without explicit programming.

4. Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is the field of developing machines that can perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence, such as reasoning and problem-solving.

5. Business Intelligence and Decision Making

Business intelligence enables organizations to analyze data effectively, providing actionable insights that support informed and strategic decision-making.

6. Real World Mini Projects

Real-world mini projects provide hands-on experience in solving practical problems using data science and technology.

7. Statistical Inference

Statistical inference is the process of drawing conclusions about a population based on data sampled from it using estimation, hypothesis testing, and confidence intervals.

8. Major Projects

Industry-oriented major projects involve tackling complex, real-world problems faced by businesses using advanced data science techniques.

9. Effective Communication and Career Readiness

Effective communication is essential for career readiness, as it enables professionals to convey ideas clearly, collaborate successfully, and build strong relationships in the workplace.
