ABOUT PRODATALYTICS

OUR STORY

Founded in 2023 by a team of data scientists and engineers, PRODATALYTICS began as a passion project to democratize data analytics. What started as a small consultancy has grown into a full-service data solutions provider serving clients across 3 continents. Our unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen helps clients bridge the gap between data and decisions.

OUR VISION

We believe data transforms lives and businesses. Our vision is to lead the data revolution by unlocking insights and creating new opportunities through cutting-edge analytics and AI solutions that drive measurable business impact.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

We constantly upgrade our platform with cutting-edge practices in AI, data science, and ML. Our tech stack includes Python, R, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Spark, and cloud platforms to ensure advanced, powerful insights for our clients.

Our Expertise

Data Science and Statistical Consulting

Our comprehensive consulting service provides end-to-end data solutions including predictive modeling, A/B testing, experimental design, and advanced analytics. We help businesses transform raw data into actionable insights using R, Python, and specialized statistical software. Our team of PhD-level statisticians ensures rigorous methodology and reproducible results.

Machine Learning Consulting

Machine Learning enables systems to improve from data and experience without explicit programming. We use NLP and ML tools to optimize and automate business operations.

Data Science Trainings

We offer hands-on training using real data, projects, and tools like R, Python, Power BI. Our goal is to help you develop practical skills for a successful data career.
